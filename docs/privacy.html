Privacy, without the fog.

This notice covers the Spillers Technology marketing website and the ComFlow demo phone line. Product deployments have their own operator and configuration.

Website visits

The website host and delivery systems may record standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, requested pages, timestamps, and errors for security and reliability.

Session replay

This site uses OpenReplay to understand navigation problems and diagnose errors. It may capture pages viewed, clicks, scrolling, browser and device details, timing data, and other interactions. Input values, email addresses, and numbers are configured to be obscured. The recorder is loaded from OpenReplay’s delivery network and session data is sent to replay.spillerstech.us.

ComFlow demo line

If you call the demo line and leave a message, the call may be recorded, transcribed, structured by automated services, and reviewed by Joseph Spillers to operate and improve the demonstration. Your phone number and anything you say may be included. Please do not leave passwords, payment information, health information, or other sensitive data.

External services and links

Typography is delivered by Google Fonts. Links to GitHub, LinkedIn, and other sites take you to services with their own privacy practices. Email and phone links use the applications and providers configured on your device.

Questions or requests

To ask what data is associated with you, request deletion where applicable, or report a privacy concern, email help@spillerstech.us. Spillers Technology may retain information when reasonably needed for security, abuse prevention, legal obligations, or an active support conversation.
